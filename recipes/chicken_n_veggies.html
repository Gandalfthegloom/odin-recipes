Chicken, Veggies and Potato Meal

Time: 15 minutes

Ingredients:

  • 150g chicken breast
  • Paprika
  • 200g White Potato
  • 30g Broccoli
  • 30g Carrots

Macros:

Protein Carbs Fat Calories
35g 47g 4.9g 396

Steps:

  1. Cut chicken into cubes or strips
  2. Add seasoning (garlic, paprika, turmeric)
  3. Steam or boil the potatoes, carrots, and broccoli
