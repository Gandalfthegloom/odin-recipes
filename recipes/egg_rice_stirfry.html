Egg and Rice Stir Fry

Time: 15 minutes

Ingredients:

  • 3 Large Eggs
  • 200g White Rice
  • 50g Cabbage (or Purple Cabbage)
  • 10ml Soya Sauce (low sodium)

Macros:

Protein Carbs Fat Calories
26g 15g 15g 575

Steps:

  1. Cook dem eggs in a pan
  2. Add pre-boiled/steamed rice
  3. Season and Stir
