Lentils.

Time: 10 Minutes

Ingredients:

  • 100g Red Lentils
  • 2 Tomatoes
  • 1/2 tsp Paprika
  • Salt & Pepper
  • 60g Green Beans

Macros:

Protein Carbs Fat Calories
28g 63g 3.8g 402

Steps:

  1. Rinse lentils, and put in a pot with boiling water
  2. Chop tomatoes, blend with some seasonings and spices.
  3. Add sauce to lentils, add some green beens, cook to finish.
